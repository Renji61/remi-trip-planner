What you can do with REMI Trip Planner

  • Create and organize trips with dates, description, cover image, and currency.
  • See trips grouped as active, draft, or archived on your personal dashboard.
  • Invite others by email or shareable link and plan together.
  • Build a day-by-day itinerary with stops, notes, optional costs, and a map with pins and routes.
  • Turn location lookup on or off when adding places.
  • Track spending against a budget, with categories and a dedicated budget view per trip.
  • Record stays, vehicle rentals, and flights with details and attachments.
  • Use reminder checklists so nothing important is forgotten.
  • Customize each trip: which sections appear, labels, layout order, and light or dark theme.
  • Add custom links in the trip sidebar for quick references.
  • Install as an app (PWA) for a more app-like experience on your device.
